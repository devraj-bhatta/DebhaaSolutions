Privacy Policy

At Debhaa Solutions , we are fully committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it secure when you use our services including mobile application, available on the Google Play Store.


1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you use our app, we may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide directly through forms or communication features within the app.

  • Automatically Collected Information: We collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to:

    • Device Information: Information about your device, including model, operating system, and unique identifiers.

    • Usages Data: Information about how you interact with our app, such as which features you use most frequently.

    • Log Data: We collect log data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages viewed to analyze trends and improve the user experience.

  • Location Information: We may collect information about your location if you permit us to do so. This is used to enhance the app experience by delivering location-based services (if applicable). You can disable location services in your device settings at any time.


2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To ensure our app operates correctly and delivers the features you expect.

  • Personalization: To personalize your experience, including showing content or ads that are more relevant to your interests.

  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and provide support.

  • Analytics: To monitor usage patterns and improve the overall performance of the app.

  • Marketing: If you have provided your consent, we may send you promotional content and updates about new features or products.


3. Third-Party Services

We may share your data with third-party service providers in the following cases:

  • Advertising Partners: We work with third-party advertisers, such as Google AdMob and Facebook Ads, to display relevant ads within the app. These advertisers may collect and use non-personally identifiable information, including device information and app interactions, to serve targeted ads.

  • Analytics Providers: We use analytics tools such as Google Analytics and Firebase Analytics to track and understand user behavior, app usage, and performance metrics. These services collect anonymized data to help us optimize and improve the app experience.

For more details on how third-party services handle your information,

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Facebook
  • Google Analytics for Firebase


4. Data Security

4. We take the protection of your personal information seriously and employ a variety of industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse of your data. These measures include encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits.

However, while we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is entirely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage users to be mindful of the risks and take their own precautions when using online services.



5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.

  • Correction: You can request to update or correct any inaccurate or outdated information.

  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations.

  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages.


6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.



7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated version will take effect immediately upon posting. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.


If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
